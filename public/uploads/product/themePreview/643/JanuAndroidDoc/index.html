Open Facebook For developer click here

* Submit Required information and click on create App

* Go Dashboard and click on Facebook login setup

* Then click on Android

* Click on Next

* Click on Next

* Enter your package name (your app's build.gradle file.)

* And copy package name and paste in Default activity class name and add .MainActivity

* Generate Key Hashes : copy your Sha-1 key and paste in below website that give you return Key Hashes(Output (base64):)

http://tomeko.net/online_tools/hex_to_base64.php

* Copy key hashes and past in facebook developer console

* Click on save and Next

* Click on Next

* Copy your facebook_app_id and fb_login_protocol_scheme and past in app>res>values>strings.xml
